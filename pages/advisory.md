This digital collection documents the ecological impact of wind turbines on bat populations.  As such, the collection includes images of deceased, mutilated, and/or decomposing bats, as well as other material that some viewers may find disturbing or unsuitable for minors.

To support the informed and responsible use of the collection, this message is intended to help users determine whether the content is appropriate for their own use and for any minors in their care.  By proceeding, you acknowledge that you understand some materials may be graphic in nature and choose to continue at your own discretion.
